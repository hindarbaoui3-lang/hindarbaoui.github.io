My Journey

Experience & Background

My path into market research wasn't accidental — it grew from a genuine curiosity about people: how they think, what they need, and how they make decisions.

I started in quantitative research, building a strong analytical foundation. Over time, I gravitated toward qualitative methods, where I could dig beneath the numbers and uncover the human stories behind the data.

Today, I specialize in qualitative research while remaining fluent in quantitative approaches — a combination that makes my insights both deep and defensible.
